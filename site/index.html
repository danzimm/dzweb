Nice to meet you, and welcome to my site. Here’s the short version of who I am and what I think about.

I’m drawn to foundational questions in physics. For example, what is measurement and how should we understand spacetime? I like problems where physical intuition and formal precision have to work together, and where the point isn’t only to calculate but to understand what the calculation is really saying.

I’ve come at those questions from a few directions. I studied theoretical physics and mathematics, am now working on a problem in geometric measure theory, and spent a decade writing software, most recently on the Triton ML compiler team at Meta. Philosophy is part of that same picture for me. It’s another way to work carefully with concepts, assumptions, and the language we use to describe them.
